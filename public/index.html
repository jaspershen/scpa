About SCPA

斯坦福中国博士后协会（SCPA）致力于连接每一位在斯坦福奋斗的华人学者。我们希望搭建一个温暖的社区，促进大家的交流与协作。

The Stanford Chinese Postdoc Association (SCPA) is a dedicated community for all Chinese postdocs and scholars at Stanford. Our mission is to build a supportive network that fosters connection, collaboration, and growth.
